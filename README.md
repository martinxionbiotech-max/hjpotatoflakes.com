## About This Knowledge Center

This repository contains the comprehensive **Technical Documentation Knowledge Center** for Hongji Agriculture — China's leading vertically integrated potato flake and powder manufacturer. The documentation is designed to serve as a high-authority, search-engine-optimized B2B knowledge base for global food manufacturers, importers, distributors, procurement specialists, and R&D professionals.

### Our Vertically Integrated Model

```
Seed Breeding → Large-Scale Farming → Modern Storage → Industrial Processing → Global Export
```

From **seed to finished ingredient**, every stage of the supply chain is managed, controlled, and optimized by Hongji Agriculture — ensuring consistent quality, reliable year-round supply, full traceability, and superior processing performance.

## Domain Overview

### Domain 01 — Potato Variety & Breeding Science (4 documents)
Fundamental knowledge on potato varieties optimized for industrial processing. Covers the three core varieties — Atlantic, Shepody, Russet Burbank — plus comparative performance matrices and breeding methodologies.

### Domain 02 — Farming & Cultivation Management (4 documents)
Standardized cultivation protocols, sustainable farming practices, harvest timing optimization, and raw material quality grading systems. Demonstrates the agricultural expertise underpinning ingredient quality.

### Domain 03 — Storage & Raw Material Management (3 documents)
Potato storage facility design and operation, year-round supply chain management, and quality preservation during storage. Addresses the critical B2B concern of supply consistency.

### Domain 04 — Processing Technology (5 documents)
In-depth technical coverage of potato flake and powder manufacturing, drying technologies, moisture control, and color/browning management. Engineering-level detail for production professionals.

### Domain 05 — Quality Control & Testing (7 documents)
Comprehensive quality assurance framework covering physical, chemical, and microbiological testing methods, rehydration performance, Certificate of Analysis (COA) interpretation, and full batch traceability.

### Domain 06 — Product & Application (9 documents)
Complete technical datasheets for potato flakes and potato powder, product grade comparisons, application-specific usage guides (bakery, snacks, instant mash, soups/sauces), custom ingredient development, and blended solutions.

### Domain 07 — Industry Knowledge & Compliance (8 documents)
Global market intelligence, Chinese potato processing industry analysis, international quality standards comparison, export documentation, regulatory compliance (FDA, EU, FSANZ, Japan), logistics, and a comprehensive glossary.

### Domain 08 — Application Development Records (8 documents)
Real-world application development case studies covering instant mashed potato rehydration optimization, gluten-free bread formulation, extruded snack density control, cream soup viscosity/stability, meat binder water-holding capacity, seasoned snack powder flavor carriers, baby food particle refinement, and pet food palatability enhancement.

## Document Standards

- **Format:** Markdown (.md) — AI-readable, version-control friendly, easy to render on any documentation platform
- **Language:** English (B2B global audience)
- **Style:** Technical manual depth with:
  - Standardized H1/H2/H3 structure
  - Machine-readable data tables
  - Measurable technical specifications with accepted ranges
  - Method references (AOAC, AACC, ISO, ASTM, GB standards)
  - Scientific citations where applicable
- **Footer:** Every document includes a standardized footer linking to the official company website
