# About Hongji Agriculture

**Hongji Agriculture Technology Co., Ltd. (弘基农业科技有限公司)** is a China-based potato ingredient manufacturer specializing in the production, processing, and global supply of potato flakes and potato powder for food manufacturers, importers, distributors, and industrial ingredient users.

The company operates an integrated agricultural and processing model covering:
Seed Selection
↓
Potato Cultivation
↓
Modern Storage Management
↓
Industrial Processing
↓
Quality Control
↓
Global Export

This vertically integrated approach enables:

- Consistent raw material quality
- Stable year-round supply capability
- Full production traceability
- Controlled processing parameters
- Customized ingredient solutions
