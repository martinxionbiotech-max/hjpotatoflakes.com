# Hongji Agriculture — Product Grade Comparison Matrix

## Introduction

Hongji Agriculture produces a comprehensive range of dehydrated potato products covering multiple grades and specifications to meet the diverse needs of global food manufacturers. This document provides a detailed comparison matrix across our product grades, enabling procurement and R&D teams to select the optimal product for their specific application.

The product portfolio is organized along two axes:

1. **Product Type:** Potato Flakes vs. Potato Powder (60 Mesh)
2. **Grade Tier:** Standard Grade → Premium Grade → Organic/Specialty Grade

Each tier reflects increasing quality control stringency, tighter specification tolerances, and suitability for progressively demanding applications.

---

## 1. Product Grade Overview

| Grade Tier | Production Emphasis | Quality Control Level | Price Index | Applications |
|-----------|-------------------|---------------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Standard Grade** | Cost-efficient bulk production | Standard in-process & final QC | Baseline (100) | Bakery, snacks, soups, sauces, meat fillers |
| **Premium Grade** | Consistent color, finer particle control | Enhanced QC, additional color & particle tests | +15–25% | Instant mash, premium snacks, baby food, exports to EU/NA |
| **Organic Grade** | Organic-certified raw material, dedicated line | Full organic certification audit trail | +40–70% | Organic-labeled products, clean-label premium brands |
| **Specialty Grade** | Custom specification | Customer-specific QC protocol | Custom | Contract manufacturing, proprietary blends |

## 13. Decision Flow Chart

```
START: What is your target application?
│
├─ Bakery / Dough → Flakes
│   ├─ Cost-sensitive → Standard Grade
│   ├─ Premium product → Premium Grade
│   └─ Organic claim → Organic Grade
│
├─ Dry Mix / Seasoning / Batter → Powder
│   ├─ Cost-sensitive → Standard Grade (60 Mesh)
│   ├─ Premium product → Premium Grade (60 Mesh)
│   └─ Ultra-fine requirement → Ultra-Fine (80-100 Mesh)
│
├─ Instant Mash / Reconstitution → Flakes
│   ├─ Foodservice → Standard Grade
│   ├─ Retail premium → Premium Grade
│   └─ Export EU/NA → Premium Grade (low reducing sugars)
│
├─ Extruded Snacks → Powder
│   ├─ Standard collets/snacks → Standard Grade
│   ├─ Premium extruded shapes → Premium Grade
│   └─ High inclusion formulation → Specialty Grade
│
└─ Custom / Proprietary → Contact R&D
    └─ Specialty Grade (custom specification development)
```
