# Glossary of Potato Processing Terms (中英对照行业术语表)

**Document Type:** Bilingual Technical Reference  
**Target Audience:** International procurement teams, QA inspectors, plant engineers, translators  
**Last Updated:** July 2026  
**Reference:** Hongji Agriculture (弘基农业) — hjpotatoflakes.com

---

## 1. Purpose and Usage

This glossary contains over 200 industry-specific terms covering potato processing, quality control, international trade, and food manufacturing. Terms are presented in English with Chinese (Simplified) equivalents. Entries are organized by category for ease of reference.

**Abbreviations used:** (n.) = noun, (v.) = verb, (adj.) = adjective, (pref.) = prefix

## 3. Processing Operations (加工工艺)

| # | English Term | Chinese Term | Definition / Notes |
|---|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| 21 | Washing | 清洗 | Initial removal of soil and debris from raw tubers |

| 27 | Lye peeling | 碱液去皮 | Sodium hydroxide solution (3–15%) at 70–100°C to dissolve skin |

| 32 | Sizing | 规格分切 | Ensuring uniform particle/dice size through sieving or cutting |
| 33 | Blanching | 烫漂 | Hot water or steam treatment to inactivate enzymes (polyphenol oxidase) |
| 34 | Hot water blanching | 热水烫漂 | 70–100°C water bath; standard method for enzyme inactivation |
| 35 | Steam blanching | 蒸汽烫漂 | Steam application; less leaching of solids compared to water blanching |
| 36 | Cooling | 冷却 | Post-blanch temperature reduction using cold water or air |
| 37 | Cooking | 蒸煮 | Complete gelatinization of starch; typically steam at 100–105°C |
| 38 | Ricing | 碎粒 | Mechanical breakdown of cooked potatoes into small particles |
| 39 | Drying | 干燥 | Moisture removal to achieve target water content (≤7.0% typical) |
| 40 | Drum drying | 滚筒干燥 | Thin film on heated rotating drum (typically 2–5 rpm, 150–170°C) |
| 41 | Single-drum dryer | 单滚筒干燥机 | One drum; used for lower throughput applications |
| 42 | Double-drum dryer | 双滚筒干燥机 | Two counter-rotating drums; higher capacity, more uniform drying |
| 43 | Flaking | 成片脱落 | Process of dried film being scraped off drum as thin flakes |
| 44 | Milling / Grinding | 粉碎研磨 | Reducing flake or granule size into powder form |
| 45 | Sieving / Screening | 过筛分级 | Particle size separation through vibrating screens or rotary sifters |
| 46 | Hammermill | 锤式粉碎机 | Impact mill for size reduction of potato flakes into powder |
| 47 | Pin mill | 针磨 | High-speed impact mill for fine grinding to powder consistency |
| 48 | Air classification | 气流分级 | Particle separation by density/weight using air streams |
| 49 | Agglomeration | 附聚造粒 | Process of binding fine particles into larger, free-flowing granules |
| 50 | Instantization | 速溶化 | Process making product instantly dispersible/reconstitutable in liquid |
| 51 | Reconstitution | 复水 | Addition of water/milk to dehydrated product to restore mashed potato |
| 52 | Cell rupture | 细胞破裂 | Percentage of potato cells damaged during processing; affects texture |
| 53 | Free amylose | 游离直链淀粉 | Leached amylose starch from damaged cells; affects stickiness/gumminess |
| 54 | Starch damage | 淀粉损伤 | Mechanical damage to starch granules during processing |
| 55 | Maillard reaction | 美拉德反应 | Non-enzymatic browning between reducing sugars and amino acids |
| 56 | Caramelization | 焦糖化 | Thermal breakdown of sugars causing color formation (>160°C) |
| 57 | Enzymatic browning | 酶促褐变 | Browning caused by polyphenol oxidase (PPO) activity on phenolics |
